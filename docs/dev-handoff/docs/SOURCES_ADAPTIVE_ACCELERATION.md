# Sources - Adaptive Acceleration and Dynamic Learning

Use these as grounding references for the adaptive acceleration design.

1. NWEA - The zone of proximal development: the power of just right. Describes using assessment data to locate instruction in a student's ZPD and plan formative assessment.
   URL: https://www.nwea.org/blog/2025/the-zone-of-proximal-development-zpd-the-power-of-just-right/

2. NWEA Knowledge Base - What is the Zone of Proximal Development? Defines ZPD as the difference between what a student can do independently and what they can do with targeted assistance/scaffolding.
   URL: https://connection.nwea.org/s/article/What-is-the-Zone-of-Proximal-Development

3. Nature Reviews Psychology - The science of effective learning with spacing and retrieval practice. Reviews evidence on spacing, retrieval practice, and metacognition across settings.
   URL: https://www.nature.com/articles/s44159-022-00089-1

4. Educational Psychology Review - A Meta-Analytic Review of the Benefit of Spacing out Retrieval Practice Episodes on Retention. Reviews spaced retrieval practice as a strategy combining retrieval and spacing effects.
   URL: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10648-020-09572-8

5. Education Endowment Foundation - Metacognition and Self-Regulated Learning. Updated guidance report summarizing evidence-based approaches for helping pupils plan, monitor, and evaluate learning.
   URL: https://educationendowmentfoundation.org.uk/education-evidence/guidance-reports/metacognition

6. Education Endowment Foundation - Feedback. Teaching and Learning Toolkit evidence summary on feedback.
   URL: https://educationendowmentfoundation.org.uk/education-evidence/teaching-learning-toolkit/feedback

7. Acceleration Institute / Belin-Blank Center - A Nation Empowered. Research-based report on academic acceleration for advanced learners.
   URL: https://www.accelerationinstitute.org/Nation_Empowered/Order/NationEmpowered_Vol1.pdf

8. National Association for Gifted Children - Acceleration. Research-backed summary on academic acceleration, achievement, social-emotional growth, and cost-effectiveness.
   URL: https://nagc.org/acceleration

9. Frontiers in Psychology 2025 - Retrieval practice enhances learning in real primary school settings. Studies retrieval/distributed practice in primary-school learning environments.
   URL: https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2025.1632206/full
