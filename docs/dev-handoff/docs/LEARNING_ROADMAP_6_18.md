# Learning Roadmap 6-18

## Age 6-8 - Foundation

Goals:
- Read Vietnamese confidently.
- Build number sense and basic arithmetic.
- Listen and speak basic English naturally.
- Develop curiosity, emotional vocabulary, and body movement.
- Learn to sit and focus for 10-15 minutes.

Modules:
- ReadMate: read stories, retell, identify characters and feelings.
- MathSense: counting, addition, subtraction, patterns, shapes.
- SpeakLab: phonics, songs, short roleplay.
- LifeQuest: emotion check-in, chores, family values.
- CodeKid: ScratchJr-style sequencing.

AI rules:
- No open-ended unsupervised chat.
- Voice-first and parent-visible.
- AI asks questions and encourages explanation.

## Age 9-11 - Learning how to learn

Goals:
- Read longer texts and summarize.
- Write paragraphs and short essays.
- Build multiplication, division, fractions, word problems.
- Reach English A1-A2 and start B1 readiness.
- Use retrieval practice and spaced repetition.
- Complete small projects.

Modules:
- Reading ladder.
- Writing Studio.
- Math mistake notebook.
- MemoryBox.
- Scratch and simple robotics.
- Home science projects.

AI rules:
- Tutor, classmate, and reading buddy modes.
- AI may give hints, not full solutions.
- Parent weekly review required.

## Age 12-15 - Autonomy and identity

Goals:
- Become self-directed learner.
- Develop critical thinking and debate.
- Reach English B1-B2.
- Learn Python, data, AI literacy, digital safety.
- Build research and project skills.
- Strengthen resilience and social skills.

Modules:
- DebateArena.
- Python playground.
- Research mentor.
- Science lab.
- Digital citizenship.
- Project portfolio.

AI rules:
- More independent AI use allowed, with audit and policy.
- Teach-back and reflection are mandatory for important sessions.
- AI must classify support level.

## Age 16-18 - Direction and portfolio

Goals:
- Choose academic, international, vocational, or hybrid pathway.
- Build projects that demonstrate competence.
- Prepare essays, interviews, portfolios.
- Learn personal finance and career planning.
- Use AI as productivity tool ethically.

Modules:
- CareerMap.
- University/pathway planner.
- Research mentor.
- Interview simulator.
- Internship/project tracker.
- Advanced coding/AI/data modules.

AI rules:
- AI can coach, critique, simulate interviews, and review drafts.
- AI cannot write final submissions dishonestly.
- Student must disclose AI assistance when required.
