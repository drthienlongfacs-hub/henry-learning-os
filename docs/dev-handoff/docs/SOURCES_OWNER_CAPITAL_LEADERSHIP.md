# Sources - Owner / Capital Allocator / Leadership Track

Use these sources as evidence anchors. Product decisions should still be validated with the child's real learning data and parent observations.

1. OECD - PISA 2022 Results Volume IV: How Financially Smart Are Students?
   - Why it matters: financial literacy is treated as a measurable student competency related to understanding money matters.
   - URL: https://www.oecd.org/en/publications/pisa-2022-results-volume-iv_5a849c2a-en.html

2. OECD - PISA 2022 Financial Literacy Framework
   - Why it matters: defines financial literacy assessment constructs and conceptual foundations for student readiness in modern society.
   - URL: https://www.oecd.org/en/publications/pisa-2022-assessment-and-analytical-framework_dfe0bf9c-en/full-report/component-4.html

3. World Economic Forum - Future of Jobs Report 2025, Skills Outlook
   - Why it matters: identifies leadership and social influence, resilience/flexibility/agility, AI and big data, and technology literacy as rising or critical skills.
   - URL: https://www.weforum.org/publications/the-future-of-jobs-report-2025/in-full/3-skills-outlook/

4. World Economic Forum - Future of Jobs Report 2025 digest
   - Why it matters: global employer survey on job and skill transformation from 2025 to 2030.
   - URL: https://www.weforum.org/publications/the-future-of-jobs-report-2025/digest/

5. OECD Learning Compass 2030
   - Why it matters: frames education around student agency, co-agency, knowledge, skills, attitudes, and values for shaping the future.
   - URL: https://www.oecd.org/en/data/tools/oecd-learning-compass-2030.html

6. National Academies - Education for Life and Work: Developing Transferable Knowledge and Skills in the 21st Century
   - Why it matters: synthesizes evidence on deeper learning and transferable skills such as critical thinking, problem solving, communication, collaboration, and self-management.
   - URL: https://nap.nationalacademies.org/catalog/13398/education-for-life-and-work-developing-transferable-knowledge-and-skills

7. Education Endowment Foundation - Metacognition and Self-Regulated Learning guidance
   - Why it matters: supports building planning, monitoring, and evaluation habits for independent learners.
   - URL: https://educationendowmentfoundation.org.uk/education-evidence/guidance-reports/metacognition
